ABOUT DK COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND SCIENCE

DK College of Engineering is a premier institution focused on delivering quality engineering education. The college provides modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented learning programs.

The campus includes advanced infrastructure, research facilities, innovation labs, and placement support to help students achieve successful careers in engineering and technology.
